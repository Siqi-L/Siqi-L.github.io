I am a joint postdoctral researcher at the Center for Discrete Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science and the Institute for Advanced Study. I got my PhD in Computer Science at UC Berkeley in 2023, advised by Alessandro Chiesa. I received my BA in Computer Science from UC Berkeley in 2018.

I am interested in theoretical computer science in general. Most recently, I have been thinking about constructions of high-dimensional expanders, their connections to manifolds, and their applications to coding theory. Here is a short video on some of my interests.
